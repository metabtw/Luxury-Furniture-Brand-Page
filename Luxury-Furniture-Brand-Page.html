Our Story

Founded in 1985 by master craftsman Alessandro Moretti, our atelier began as a small workshop in Florence, Italy. For over three decades, we have remained dedicated to the pursuit of perfection in furniture design and craftsmanship.

Today, our collections can be found in luxury residences, boutique hotels, and prestigious establishments around the world, each piece bearing the hallmark of our uncompromising commitment to quality.

"We don't simply create furniture; we craft heirlooms that tell stories across generations."

— Alessandro Moretti, Founder
Our Craftsmanship

Our Journey

1985

Founded in Florence, Italy as a small artisanal workshop specializing in handcrafted wooden furniture.

1997

Expanded operations and opened our first international showroom in Paris, marking our entry into the global luxury market.

2008

Introduced our signature "Eternità" collection, which received international acclaim and established our distinctive design language.

2022

Launched our sustainable luxury initiative, committing to environmentally responsible practices while maintaining our standards of excellence.

The Art of Creation

Each piece begins with carefully selected materials—the finest woods, leathers, fabrics, and metals—chosen not only for their beauty but for their durability and character.

Our craftsmen employ both time-honored techniques passed down through generations and innovative methods that push the boundaries of what's possible in furniture design.

From the initial sketch to the final polish, every step in our creation process is executed with precision and care, resulting in pieces that are not just furniture, but works of art designed to be cherished for generations.
